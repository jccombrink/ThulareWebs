Information


Planning your trip in advance is of utmost importance to guarantee a succesful safari. The information below is there to assist you with exactly that. Should any additional information be required do not hesitate to contact us.

Importation of weapons to South Africa

We require you to send us copies of the following documents ahead of the hunt. Bring copies and original documents with you when you travel to South Africa.

• Copy of flight ticket

• Copy of passport

• Copy of rifle license/ownership/permit

• Copy of proof of export from country of origin

• Your full name, surname, physical address, date of birth.


When you arrive in South Africa

We will send you an invitation letter that must be taken with you to SA.
Upon arrival you can collect your luggage and proceed to the arrivals terminal. Your rifle will be at the SAPS firearm desk.
I will meet you at the terminal and take you to the SAPS firearm desk to collect your rifle.
We will send you an SAPS 520 rifle import document by e-mail. This hould be filled in with black ink but not signed before traveling to SA.
The signing of the SAPS 520 document will be conducted before the SAPS official inspecting the rifles and issuing the import license.

Firearms and Ammunition

We recommend you bring your rifles (with scopes), but a selection of firearms will be available for hire on prior request.

• A minimum calibre of .270 is required for plains game.

• A minimum calibre of .375 is required for larger game and big 5.

• Solid ammunition of at least 300 grain is required for elephant and buffalo.

Trophies

We despatch trophies to a taxidermist when required. We make use of Lifeform Taxidermy (www.lifeformtaxidermy.com) for mounting and Gametan (www.gametan.co.za) for raw trophy exports. If the client wishes to use his own taxidermist or export agent in SA then it will be arranged that it be sent from our taxidermist to that taxidermist at the clients cost. All correspondence in connection with the trophies shall be directed to Lifeform Taxidermy and Gametan.

Travel

The closest international airport to us is Oliver Tambo International Airport. Pick up and drop-off from this airport is already included in daily fees. For transport from another airport we will quote seperately.

Make sure your airlines transport firearms.

Report rifle during your booking with airlines

Climate

Hunting takes place from May to October. Temperatures are between 10&celsius; - 20. Days are pleasantly warm but nights are cold.

Clothing

Clothes such as khaki and green. For evenings and early mornings warmer clothes is recommended, escpecially during July and August. Remember comfortable hunting boots (soft-soled type) and hats. A pair of binoculars will also come in handy. Bring along personal items such as sunglasses, special medication and sunblock. Washing of clothes is included in the daily fees.

Accomodation

Accomodation consists of 2 fully furnished chalets with en-suite bathrooms. Chalets are close to the kitchen, dining room, outside lapa (braai area) and overlooking a dam. Chalets are serviced daily.

Meals

Breakfast, lunch and dinner is included in the daily fees together with bottled water, soft drinks, beers and wine. Meals are prepared the traditional South African way
