Thulare means to save in a time of abundance so that you have enough in a time of hardship.
Thulare was an African king that taught his nation to lay aside the extra grain in the good years for the harder years to come.
Thulare Hunting Safaris aims to also then save for the future, to hunt selectively and keep the best genes going forward.
Thulare Safaris operates in a malaria free area near Burgersfort in the Limpopo province.

The farm is 10 000 acres of magnificent bushveld and is well known for its excellent quality of Kudu, Njala and Mountain Reedbuck to name a few.

Once you and your proffesional hunting guide are stalking the trophy of a lifetime, you will feel your heart pounding and find yourself becoming part of the ancient rythm of Africa

Our camp is a four hour drive north of the Oliver Tambo International Airport and are about 100 miles from the well known Kruger National Park.
