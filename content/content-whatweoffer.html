Thulare Safaris offers an unbeatable experience in the bushveld, complete with south african meals, sounds and wildlife...

We do our best to accomodate your needs and provide you with prompt and accurate information. Hunts are conducted with skill, ethics, perseverance and dedication. Our range of game species are comprehensive and of good quality.

At Thulare Safaris we offer both rifle and bow hunting. Hunting season for rifle starts in April and for bow in July. Hunting season ends around September.
Our authentic South African accomodation and meals are of good quality and give you that genuine african experience.
The entire farm is exclusively booked out for you or your group during your stay.

Below is the basic information you will need to plan your trip, for everything else head over to our Information page.


Daily rates and Conditions 2017

Plains game hunting daily fees per person in Euro (€)

1 Hunter : 1 Proffesional Hunter € 260

2 Hunters : 1 Proffesional Hunter € 190 each

Non-Hunter € 100



Booking terms

A deposit of 50% will confirm your booking. Deposits must be done electronically to the banking details provided. The other 50% is payable on the first day of the hunt. Trophy fees are payable on the last day of the hunt.


Daily rates include:

Pick up and drop-off at Oliver Tambo International Airport

Accomodation (see here)

Breakfast and Dinner

Drinks (bottled water, soft drinks, beers and wine)

The service of a PH, trackers and skinners

All hunting licenses

Washing of clothes


Daily rates does not include:

Arranging of visas and passports

Flights to and from Oliver Tambo International Airport

Trophy fees

Hiring of firearms and/or ammunition (€ 20 per rifle per hunter per day, and ammunition as per SA price)

Taxidermy, packing, insurance and export costs

Hotel costs for before and after the hunt




Per request we offer day-trips to the Kruger National Park. This is at a cost of € 200 per person and includes transport to and from the park, meals and a guide to show you the very best places the Kruger has to offer.
