Accomodation


Thulare Safaris boasts a beautiful hunting camp next to a small dam in the middle of the bushveld. A spectacular wild fig tree canopies over the outside lapa and provides ample bird watching opportunities.
The African night sky is an absolute must see and a regular visit by the African Wood Owl makes one feel the intensity of the African Bushveld.


The camp has two chalets for guests, a lounge, outside lapa, dining room and fully equipped kitchen to which guests have full access. The two chalets can accomodate two guests each. Each chalet comes with an on-suite bathroom and is fully furnished. The rooms are serviced daily, and laundry services are included.


Both of these chalets have a beautiful view over the dam where game often appear in the afternoons to have a drink.

*Accomodation prices are already included in the daily rates*
